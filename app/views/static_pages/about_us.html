About Advocates for Free-Range Turkeys

We love turkeys! Especially eating them! We feel that when turkeys are allowed to roam freely, they taste much better. Our organization is fighting to end mass turkey farms and to gain better living environments for our fine-feathered friends.

Created for Dr. Sessions, PhD and billionaire, by Charleston Southern students.
